Pork Steak

Home

Description

The pork steak is one of the simplest dish, especially when frying.Just make sure that the hot plate is at medium.

  • Pork steak
  • Butter
  • Spices

Ingridients

  1. Put spices inside a clean bowl.
  2. Dip the steak inside the spices.
  3. Wait roughly for 15 minutes.
  4. switch on the hot plate, the hot plate must be medium.
  5. place a pan on top of the hot plate
  6. Put the butter inside the pan until it melts
  7. Fry the steat for roughly 7 minutes on one side n roughly 7 minutes on the other side.
